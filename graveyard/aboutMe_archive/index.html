I am a 2023 alumnus of SUNY Oswego, presently working with Dr. Caitlin Hudac on the Social Connection and Well-Being (SCWB) study at the University of South Carolina.

My academic and professional journeys are fueled by unlimited curiosity and passions. My current research interests can be plainly defined by this inquiry: "What is it like?" I hope to get my PhD and research how identity/identities (LGBTQ+ belonging, race, gender, ethnicity, socio-economic status, etc.) may intersect and affect social behaviors.
