Hey there! My name is Ezra, and I am a 2023 alumnus of SUNY Oswego.

I'm currently working as a Research Assistant working with Dr. Caitlin Hudac on the Social Connection and Well-Being (SCWB) at the University of South Carolina. I am very interested in research, with a focus on how identities (LGBTQ+ belonging, race, gender, ethnicity, socio-economic status, etc.) may intersect and affect our social cognition. I am also extremely interested in Cognitive Science, Computer Science, and Computational Neuroscience. Most of my neuroscience-based interests can be summed up by the following inquiry: "What is it like?"
