Hey there! My name is Ezra, and I am a Digital Library Analyst for the Lowcountry Digital Library (LCDL) at the College of Charleston.

I previously worked as a Research Assistant with Caitlin Hudac on the Social Connection and Well-Being (SCWB) Study, and I am a 2023 alumnus from SUNY Oswego.
